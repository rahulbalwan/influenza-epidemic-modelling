## Conceptual note

The methodologies applied in this project are grounded in classical infectious disease modelling, combining:

- exponential growth theory  
- reproduction number estimation  
- compartmental models (SEIR, SIRS)  
- seasonal forcing dynamics  

to understand influenza as a **recurrent seasonal epidemic system**.
